Our Collaborators

ARUA

Partnering with ARUA to advance research excellence and collaboration among African universities, tackling continental challenges.

AAU

Working with the Association of African Universities (AAU) to foster academic partnerships and promote higher education development across Africa.

RUFORUM

We collaborate with RUFORUM to strengthen higher education and research capacity in agriculture and sustainable development across African universities.

ACE IMPACT

Partnering with ACE IMPACT to enhance postgraduate education and research in STEM, fostering innovation and development in Africa.

OCP Africa

Working with OCP Africa to empower smallholder farmers through innovative agricultural solutions and training programs tailored to African contexts.

FAO

Collaborating with FAO to address food security and sustainable agriculture challenges, promoting resilience in African communities.

Agripreneur Alliance

Collaborating with Agripreneur Alliance to support young agricultural entrepreneurs, driving innovation and sustainability in agribusiness.

RESCIF

Collaborating with RESCIF on tackling the fundamental issues in the future including: water, energy and nutrition.

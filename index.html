Welcome to the UM6P - Africa Initiative

Based at UM6P in Benguerir, Morocco, the Africa Initiative Department is dedicated to contributing to Africa’s socio-economic development. We support collaboration, foster dialogue around innovative futures, and help strengthen UM6P’s role in higher education, research for development (R4D), innovation, and entrepreneurship across the continent.

Our Mission

Contribute to strengthening UM6P’s role in higher education, research for development (R4D), innovation, and entrepreneurship through collaborative partnerships, support for OCP actions on the ground, and interdepartmental coordination in response to Africa’s socio-economic development needs.

Strategic Partnerships

Facilitating networks with African and global institutions to support UM6P’s contributions and extend its reach.

Innovation Hub

Encouraging visionary ideas and dialogue to help shape Africa’s future through UM6P’s ecosystem.

Continental Focus

Engaging with Africa’s challenges through collaborative, localized solutions.

What We Do

Aligning Research Agendas

We help connect UM6P’s research for development (R4D) with Africa’s socio-economic priorities and the SDGs, fostering collaboration to contribute to addressing challenges like food security and sustainable energy.
